## Scenario 1: Ordering 엽기떡볶이 for the First Time

**Customer:**  
안녕하세요! 엽기떡볶이를 주문하고 싶은데요. 매운 거 잘 못 먹어서 덜 맵게 해주실 수 있나요?  
(Hi! I'd like to order 엽기떡볶이. I can't handle spicy food well, so can you make it less spicy?)

**Server:**  
네, 덜 매운 맛으로 해드릴게요. 덜매운맛은 불닭볶음면만큼 매운데, 괜찮으시겠어요?  
(Sure, I'll make it less spicy. The Less Spicy level is about as spicy as 불닭볶음면, is that okay for you?)

**Customer:**  
네, 괜찮아요.  
(Yes, that's fine.)

**Server:**  
주 재료는 떡, 오뎅, 반반, 분모자가 있습니다. 어떤 걸로 하시겠어요?  
(We have main ingredients options: rice cake, fish cake, half-and-half, or konjac. Which one would you like?)

**Customer:**  
떡으로 할게요.  
(I'll go with rice cake.)

**Server:**  
소스는 엽기(오리지널), 로제(덜매움), 짜장, 마라 중에서 선택하실 수 있습니다. 어떤 소스로 드릴까요?  
(For the sauce, you can choose from Original, Rose (less spicy), Jjajang, and Mala. Which one would you like?)

**Customer:**  
로제로 주세요.  
(Rose, please.)

**Server:**  
알겠습니다. 엽기떡볶이는 양이 많아서 보통 3-4명이 같이 먹어요. 주먹밥이나 튀김 세트도 함께 주문하시겠어요?  
(Got it. 엽기떡볶이 comes in a large portion, usually shared by 3-4 people. Would you like to order some rice balls or fried snacks as well?)

**Customer:**  
그럼요, 주먹밥 세트랑 치즈 추가해주세요. 치즈랑 같이 먹으면 더 맛있다고 들었어요.  
(Of course, please give us the rice ball set and add cheese. I've heard it tastes even better with cheese.)

**Server:**  
알겠습니다. 단무지나 쿨피스도 같이 드릴까요? 쿨피스는 매운 맛을 중화시키는 데 좋아요.  
(Got it. Would you like some pickled radish or Coolpis with it? Coolpis is great for balancing the spiciness.)

**Customer:**  
쿨피스 주세요. 엽기떡볶이 진짜 기대돼요!  
(Coolpis, please. I'm really looking forward to trying 엽기떡볶이!)
